## Reporting a Vulnerability
If you discover a vulnerability or sensitive data exposure:

1. **Do not** open a public issue.  
2. Email **ochoa104cy@outlook.com** with subject line “Vulnerability Disclosure.”  
3. Provide:
   - Steps to reproduce  
   - Expected vs. actual behavior  
   - Potential impact assessment

Reports are triaged within **72 hours**. Confirmed issues will be patched and disclosed responsibly.
