TANO Framework

Integrates cybersecurity and data science into a measurable methodology for trustworthy systems.

Core Principles

  • Trust: Integrity through transparent systems.
  • Accuracy: Data-driven precision and validation.
  • Neutrality: Balanced interpretation and governance.
  • Organization: Structured workflows and alignment.
  • Transparency: Open and verifiable processes.
