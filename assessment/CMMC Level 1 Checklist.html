CMMC Level 1 Checklist

TANO = Trust • Accuracy • Neutrality • Organization • Transparency

For small organization - No prior experience.

Who this applies to:

Small businesses that ONLY handle FCI (Federal Contract Information) and are subject to FAR 52.204-21.

Goal:

Implement 15 basic cybersecurity practices and be able to self attest.

Phase 0 - Before You Start (Required Context)

    Confirm you are Level 1
    • You do NOT handle CUI
    • You DO handle FCI
    • Your contract references FAR 52.204-21
    • You are NOT required to get third-party certified
    Assign responsibility
    • One person is responsible for cybersecurity (can be IT, owner, or MSP)
    • This person maintains evidence
    Identify what systems are in scope
    • Computers used for work
    • Email systems
    • Network equipment (router Wi-Fi)
    • File storage (cloud or local)

Phase 1 - Access Control (AC)

AC.L1-3.1.1 - Limit access to authorized users

    Each employee has their own account
    No shared logins
    Accounts are disabled when someone leaves
  • Evidence: user list, screenshots, or policy
