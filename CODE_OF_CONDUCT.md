# Contributor Code of Conduct

We pledge to maintain a productive, inclusive, and respectful environment for all contributors.

- **Be respectful:** No harassment, discrimination, or disruptive behavior.  
- **Be transparent:** Communicate decisions clearly; document major changes.  
- **Be ethical:** Use research and tools only for lawful, authorized, and educational purposes.  
- **Report concerns:** Contact ochoa104cy@outlook.com for any incident reports.

Violations may result in loss of contributor privileges or removal from collaboration.
